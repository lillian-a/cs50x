Breakout - The Game

How To Play:

Move The Paddle:
You can either use the Left and Right arrow keys on the keyboard or hover your mouse over the game of the direction (left or right) of where you want to move.


Goal Of The Game:
Hit the bricks with the ball while not letting it hit the bottom. You start with 3 lives, each time the ball hits the bottom a life is lost. 3 Lives Lost = Game Over.

To Start the Game:
Click inside of the game window.
